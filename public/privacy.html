Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TokScheduler ("we", "our", or "the Application") collects, uses, stores, and shares information in connection with your use of our local software application and its integrations, specifically the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Data Collection and Usage

TokScheduler is a local-first software application designed to run privately on your own computer. We collect only the data necessary to provide scheduling and posting functionalities:

  • TikTok Profile Data: When you connect your TikTok account via OAuth, we retrieve your user ID, profile name, display handle, and profile avatar image URL. This is used solely to identify your connected account and customize the dashboard interface.
  • Media Files: We store the video files (.mp4) that you manually upload to the scheduling queue. These files are stored in a local directory on your machine (`uploads/`) and are used solely to transmit the content to TikTok's publishing servers.
  • Access Credentials: The application stores OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens received from TikTok. These credentials are used to sign and authenticate API requests to TikTok.

2. Data Storage and Retention

All collected data is stored locally on the device where you run the application (in `config.json` and `posts.json`). We do not host central servers, and your data is never sent to any developer-owned or third-party servers, with the sole exception of the official TikTok API endpoints (`open.tiktokapis.com` and related upload CDNs).

Media files are deleted from your local device immediately upon successful publication to TikTok or if you manually delete the post from the scheduling queue.

3. Data Sharing and Transfer

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data. Your data is sent directly from your local computer to TikTok APIs to facilitate the content scheduling and video posting features requested by you. This data transfer is subject to TikTok's own Developer Policy and Terms.

4. User Rights and Data Deletion

Since your information is stored entirely on your own local storage, you have absolute control over your data. You can delete all tokens, profile info, and scheduled videos at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Disconnect Account" button in the dashboard, which immediately wipes all stored TikTok access and refresh tokens.
  • Deleting the `config.json`, `posts.json`, and the contents of the `uploads/` folder in the application root directory.

5. Compliance with Children's Online Privacy Protection

The Application is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or data security within the Application, please contact the developer via your designated platform support options.
